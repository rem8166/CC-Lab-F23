Panda Dance Journey!

join us on a beautiful journey of how a panda learned to dance against all odds

In this world, pandas are not allowed to dance because they are too scared and insecure. One day, a brave panda decided he would learn to dance and that his unsupportive mean grumpy papa wouldn't stop him.

By following the instructions on the screen given by dance guru Charis, an eclectic vineyard-owning greek bear wearing a beret, panda will learn how to dance, slowly and with a lot of effort, this panda learns how to dance.

This is a a beautiful story of an underdog beating all odds and overcoming mental barriers and succeeding in learning how to dance. Everyone should learn from him!
